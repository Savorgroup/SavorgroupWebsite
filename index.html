About Us

Savor Group began its journey in 2014 with a cosy café tucked away in the vibrant alleyways of Jonker Street Melaka, Backlane Coffee. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to quality, we expanded our offerings to include the refreshing delights of Heesan. Building upon our success, we ventured into the realm of hotpot with Little Fat Pot 小肥锅. Continuing our journey of culinary exploration, we proudly introduce Yakiniku Shin, our latest establishment that celebrates the art of Japanese barbecue.

At Savor Group, we are dedicated to providing enjoyable dining experiences that showcase the richness of flavors and the beauty of culinary craftsmanship. With each establishment, we strive to create welcoming spaces where friends and family gather to savor moments of joy and connection.

We look forward to welcoming you to our establishments and sharing the essence of Savor Group with you.
